INKSCAPE SPECIALS:
CTRL+D  	duplicate 
CTRL+G   	group
1       	100% zoom
5       	Center canvas, fill screen
+ & -   	Zoom in & zoom out
PgUp & PgDn	Move up & down stack 1 step
Home & End	Move to top and bottom of stack

====
Inkscape setting
- View = custom;
- Zoom = 1:1;
- Align&Distribution = {Relative:LastSelected};
- File&Strokes = {};
